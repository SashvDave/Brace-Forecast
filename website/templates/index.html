Oral Hygiene Tips

Brushing

  1. Practice good technique.
  2. Brush your teeth for two minutes.
  3. Keep your equipment clean.
  4. Try to keep it separate from other toothbrushes in the same holder to prevent cross-contamination.
  5. Invest in a new toothbrush every three months

Flossing

  1. Don't rush.
  2. Don't snap the floss into your gums. When the floss reaches your gum line, curve it against one tooth, making a c shape.
  3. Use the floss to gently rub the side of the tooth in an up-and-down motion.
  4. If you find it hard to handle the floss, try using a water flosser.
